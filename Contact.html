We’re Here to Help

Refunds, payments, delivery and ticket orders

▼

Songkick provides ticket links but doesn’t sell tickets.

For any issues like refunds or order changes, please contact the vendor directly.

Check your bank statement or email for vendor details, or visit the Songkick event listing for more info.

Add concerts to website with Tourbox widget

▼

To add concerts to your website, submit the 'Add Concert' form on our site.

Select the event type (concert, festival, or live stream), fill in the required details (headliner, date, venue), and click 'Save Concert.'

You're all set!

Add your artist to Songkick

▼

If your artist isn’t on Songkick yet, no worries!

After setting up your Tourbox account, search for your artist.

If they aren’t listed, you’ll be able to add them as a new artist.

Add a biography to your Songkick artist page

▼

To add a bio for your artist, simply send us the copy and we’ll update it on our site.

The bio should be between 225 and 1000 words and must be unique—no duplicate content from the web.

User accounts and artist pages on Songkick

▼

On Songkick, usernames and artist names are separate.

Create a personal account with any username to manage your artist pages on Tourbox, our platform for managing concerts across the web.
